Key features

This tool provides you features that help you create a perfect level for you.

Live preview with tile board

Making levels requires a preview board game to help designers see how it would appear in the game.

Level Editor now supports themes

For the better experience of making levels, themes option offers designers to enhance the editor style, making it easier to interact.

Testing your level in Candy Crush right in your browser

Live preview is just your level appearance, but testing your level in the game is the most important part of the process.

Connect with friends in Community

Make your levels, but don't forget to share your work to the community, where you can get feedback and learn from others.

Save your work and get back to your level whenever you want, ...without account

Level Editor saves your level to your browser data, which means on every reload page your level and progress will still be there. Your level will be deleted if you clear your browser data.
